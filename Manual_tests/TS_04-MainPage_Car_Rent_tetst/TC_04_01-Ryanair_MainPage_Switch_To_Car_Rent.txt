Title - TC_04_01-Ryanair_MainPage_Switch_To_Car_Rent
Description - Test case that tests if navigation to car rent works as expected

Pre-conditions
  1) Operating system at least Windows 10 or macOS Sonoma 14.3.1
  2) Internet connection available
  3) Open website https://www.ryanair.com/cz/cs
  4) Mouse, keyboard and display available
  5) Refuse cookies by clicking the button "Ne děkuji"

Steps
  1) In the top menu click the "Naplánovat" button
  2) In the pop-up click on the right side on "Vyhledat pronájem auta"

Expected result
  Car rent form is present

Version - 1.0
